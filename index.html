About Me

I love to code because it is usable art.
You can create something and people interact with, use, touch and work with.
That is an amazing feeling.

I was born in Milan, Italy, in 1988.
Graduated in Literature and Philosophy, for the last 14 years I have traveled the world for pleasure and for work, mainly dealing with art and live entertainment.
Experience in the world of theater and circus has taught me that there is no limit to human potential and to communicate with people of all languages ​​and nationalities .
Since 2014 I have been creating and managing the websites of various circus companies.
This showed me the infinite possibilities of the web: digital communication is indispensable for me to transform, innovate and grow any human activity.
