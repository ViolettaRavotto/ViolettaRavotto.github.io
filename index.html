Some questions you might have in your mind...

A quality website is important for a business. ...

Looking good is important because your website is often your first impression you give potential customers. If they found you through a search or through a link a friend gave them, your website will be your first point of contact with them.

One of the main reasons you should have a website for your business is to increase your organization's credibility. Without a website, people may question your legitimacy as a business.

Having a professional website is an opportunity to make a great first impression and give people comfort that you're a real business. (Forbes, 2020/02/03)

Investing in a quality website can increase your business, while cutting costs and going for the cheaper option can hurt you more than it can help you!

How much you have to invest to create your website will on its size, features, and complexity.

Designing and building your portfolio or showcase website cost around 700 euro.

You have also to purchase your domain name (usually between 10E and 20E for year) and website hosting plan (around 100E for year).
